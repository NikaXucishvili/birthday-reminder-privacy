Privacy Policy – Birthday Reminder

Last updated: March 16, 2026

1. Information We Access

This app may request access to your device contacts solely to import birthday information at your request.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • All data is stored locally on your device only
  • No data is collected, transmitted, or shared with any third party
  • Contacts are never uploaded to any server
  • Birthday data you enter is stored only on your device

3. Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device information and identifiers to show relevant ads. Please review Google's privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. User Control

  • You can revoke contacts permission anytime in device Settings
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data

5. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
